Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about library cards, borrowing, and services.

Library Cards and Borrowing

How do I get a library card?

Visit the circulation desk with a valid photo ID and proof of residence (e.g., a utility bill). Cards are free for all local residents.

How long can I keep a book?

Most books can be borrowed for three weeks (21 days).

Can I renew items?

Yes! You can renew eligible items twice online through your Account page or by calling the library.
