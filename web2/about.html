Our Mission and History

The Beginner Library was founded in 1985 with a commitment to providing free access to information and lifelong learning for all members of the community. We believe in the power of literacy and the importance of a welcoming space for discovery.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Ensuring everyone has easy access to our resources.
  • Community: Serving as a central hub for local engagement and activities.
  • Learning: Promoting continuous education and intellectual growth.
